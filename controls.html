Controls

Overwatch is a game that is on PC, Xbox One, and Playstation 4. Being on different consoles means that your controls will vary depending on what system you play on, so here we will go over the default controls for each platform.

Xbox One

  • Left Stick: Move
  • Right Stick: Aim
  • A: Jump
  • B: Toggle Crouch
  • X: Reload
  • RB: Ability 1
  • LB: Ability 2
  • Y: Ability 3
  • RT: Primary Fire
  • LT: Alternate Fire
  • D-Pad Right: Next Weapon
  • R3: Melee

Playstation 4

  • Left Stick: Move
  • Right Stick: Aim
  • Cross: Jump
  • Circle: Toggle Crouch
  • Square: Reload
  • R1: Ability 1
  • L1: Ability 2
  • Triangle: Ability 3
  • R2: Primary Fire
  • L2: Alternate Fire
  • D-Pad Right: Next Weapon
  • R3: Melee

PC

  • W: Forward
  • A: Left
  • S: Back
  • D: Right
  • Move Mouse: Aim
  • Space: Jump
  • Left Control: Toggle Crouch
  • R: Reload
  • Left Shift: Ability 1
  • E: Ability 2
  • Q: Ability 3
  • Left Click: Primary Fire
  • Right Click: Alternate Fire
  • Mouse-Wheel Up: Next Weapon
  • V: Melee
