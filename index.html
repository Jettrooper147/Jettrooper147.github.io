Welcome to Overwatch!

Overwatch is a first-person shooter game, with class based, team oriented gameplay. Developed by Blizzard and released on May 24th, 2016, this new title from Blizzard has become very popular, currently having over 30 million players. If you've just picked up the game or are considering buying it, this website is designed for you to be able to quickly and easily get information about the characters and how they work, maps and game modes, controls for your preffered system, and how to unlock cool items for your characters!
